## What BLIPS is

BLIPS is a premium direct-to-consumer apparel brand. India. Urban professionals. Rs.1,299+. Manufacturing: Tirupur, Tamil Nadu. Store: blipsstore.com.

**Tagline:** "You're just a blip in the vast universe. Act accordingly."

**Philosophy:** Cosmic humility. Personal responsibility. Observational. Slightly sarcastic. Calmly confrontational.

**Positioning:** Concept-first. Not a lifestyle brand with attitude — a brand with a philosophical position. Premium philosophical identity brand. Not meme merch. Not motivational gym wear. Clothing for self-aware adults navigating pressure.

**Core tension that powers the brand:** "You don't matter in the grand scheme. But your choices matter in your lifetime." That paradox is the brand.

BLIPS is **not**:
- Text-forward. Concept drives form. Design can be pure typography, pure visual, combined, or abstract.
- A fast fashion brand. Intentional drops. Cult brand dynamics.
- Generic streetwear. The depth is in the decade architecture, not the aesthetic.

## Brand Voice — the rule that doesn't bend

**Observational. Slightly sarcastic. Calmly confrontational. Never desperate. Never preachy.**

> Blips doesn't shout. It smirks.

**Tone rule:** Roast *with* them — never roast *at* them. *"You and I both know this is ridiculous."* Not: *"You're pathetic."*

**Editorial register:** Stop over-dramatizing. Stop under-living. Zoom out. But act anyway. Statistically insignificant. So don't think so much — buy a t-shirt.

**Voice tests for any BLIPS output (manifestation copy, brief language, campaign line, hang tag):**

1. Would the reader feel seen, not sold to? (If sold-to, rewrite.)
2. Is there a smirk underneath? (If earnest-without-irony, rewrite.)
3. Could this be a LinkedIn motivational post? (If yes, rewrite — that voice is the anti-BLIPS.)
4. Does it name the trap, or just describe the situation? (Naming the trap is the BLIPS move.)
5. Could a 28-year-old urban professional in Chennai recognise themselves in it without flinching? (If it sounds like a stereotype, rewrite.)

## Typography (LOCKED)

| Role | Font | Weight | Use |
|------|------|--------|-----|
| Display / wordmark | **Syne** | 800 (headers, wordmark) · 600 (section headers) | Identity, primary headings |
| Editorial / campaign | **Cormorant Garamond** | 300 italic | Sparingly — philosophical statements, taglines, hang tags |
| Functional / system | **DM Mono** | 300, 400 | Body text, labels, data, tags, UI copy |

Hierarchy comes from size and weight deltas — not colour. A heading is bigger/heavier than body. Secondary text is smaller/dimmer. Muted text is smaller still. All stay within the five-token tonal palette.

## Product Philosophy (LOCKED)

- **Concept-first.** Signal → Concept → Design. Form follows meaning.
- **Climate-first garments.** Designed for India's tropical heat (38–42°C Chennai). NOT adapted Western silhouettes.
  - Open collar architecture
  - Underarm ventilation panels
  - Extended half-sleeves
- **INDIAsize anthropometric data** (26,000 Indians, 6 cities) identified for sizing — integrated at first tech pack stage.
- **Story-based drops.** Theme first. Designs match the theme. Not generic collections.
- **Price:** Rs.1,299+ (gap between Snitch Rs.699-899 and Rare Rabbit Rs.1,800+).
- **Cult brand model:** Supreme / Corteiz. Access earned. Rules felt. Best customers discovered, not acquired.
- **Target (28-38) will NOT engage with polls, surveys, or validation asks.** Pre-validation is rejected as a strategy. The brand earns its position through made objects, not customer-development theatre.

## BLIPS Standard Garment Spec (LOCKED — March 8, 2026)

Base construction spec for **all** BLIPS garments across all three decades. FURNACE briefs reference this section — they don't repeat it. Only signal-specific deviations go in the brief.

### Fabric
- 240 GSM ring-spun combed cotton
- Side-seam construction (no centre-back seam)
- Enzyme wash post-construction — broken-in feel on day one

### Silhouette
- Set-in shoulder — sleeve holds the arm symmetrically, does not drop
- NOT drop shoulder. This is locked. Do not revisit.

### Sleeve
- Mid-bicep length — climate-appropriate, elevated vs basic tee
- Locked March 8, 2026. Do not revisit.

### Collar
- Open crew — wider neck opening (+1.5cm vs standard), lowered back
- Airflow by design, not as an afterthought
- NOT a standard crew neck. The difference is intentional and felt immediately.

### Underarm
- Open-weave cotton gusset panel at primary heat-exit point
- Invisible externally — functional, not decorative
- Climate innovation specific to Indian tropical wear

### Hem
- Double-needle, weighted tape — flat hang, no roll

### Interior label
- Woven tape, DM Mono — "BLIPS / blipsstore.com"
- No printed label. Woven only.

### Badge
- BLIPS embroidery, bottom left or right chest
- Small, clean, Syne 800 wordmark — no pulsing dot at this scale
- Tone-on-tone always: thread colour matched to season palette
- BLIPS mark never on garment body. Badge and hem label only.

### Print (default — overridden per brief if needed)
- Flat screen print
- Ink colours specified per signal brief

## Engine Room Pipeline (LOCKED)

Six stages. Raw signal in. Finished product out. Nothing skips a stage.

1. **BUNKER** — Signal captured.
2. **STOKER** — Decade resonance scored. Manifestations produced per resonant decade (1-3 of RCK / RCL / RCD).
3. **FURNACE** — Brand fit per manifestation + design brief.
4. **BOILER** — Concept gallery + approved-concept mockup render.
5. **ENGINE** — Tech pack from approved mockup.
6. **PROPELLER** — Vendor bundle + production order.

ORC orchestrates. Six skills below ORC, each loaded per pipeline stage. Master Sheet (Engine Room Master in Drive) = single source of truth pre-BOS migration; the Postgres + Inngest pipeline is the source of truth as of Phase 5+.
